Research Events

Some nights, the group votes to go deeper. That’s when we host **Research Events** with our partner ELDAEON—bringing purposeful capabilities while keeping everything consent-first and human-centered.

What changes on a Research Event?

Aligned capabilities

  • Low-light, time-synced video & observation notes
  • Environmental readings (EMF, magnetic, acoustic, atmospheric)
  • Sky context (satellite/plane passes, meteor windows)
  • Optional coherence markers (e.g., HRV) with opt-in summaries

Consent & options

  • Clear pre-brief & opt-in checkboxes (by capability)
  • Camera-free zones, signal cards, and a “pause” hand sign
  • Right to withdraw at any time—no questions asked

Aftercare & sharing

  • Plain-language summary within a reasonable timeframe
  • Optional personal note review (upon request)
  • No public release of individual data without explicit consent

Why ELDAEON?

Because they’re brilliant, careful, and values-aligned. ELDAEON.com blends rigorous instrumentation with a deep respect for human experience. Think “scientist-poets with great gear.”
