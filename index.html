The Experience

Three hours. Esoteric, gentle, and clear. We settle the body, tune the mind, meet the sky, and share what arose—without hype.

  • Arrival & Grounding (60m): welcome, orientation, nervous-system downshift.
  • Group Coherence (30m): simple guided practice; shared timing; headphones optional.
  • Star Quiet (≈90m): skywatching, inner noticing, light journaling.
  • Close & Compare (few m): kind reflections—what we felt, saw, or learned.

Code of Care

Fun, open, and safe. We don’t claim to know exactly what’s happening—and that’s okay. We build warm containers so whatever happens, happens kindly.

Safety with a smile

Clear boundaries, clear cues. Substance-free nights, consent-first interactions, trauma-informed facilitation—refined through years of skywatching projects.

Try it at Home (mini)

Want a taste? Here’s a soft, sovereign starter.

  1. Set the tone: your words, your way—gratitude or a nod to higher wisdom.
  2. Press play: headphones on; start the Epiphany Awaits playlist.
  3. Soften: breathe; let your body feel more, mind do less.
  4. Look up: no forcing—notice felt sense, images, nudges.
  5. Note it: a word, a sketch, a feeling—enough to remember.

The playlist is the ride; you just allow.
