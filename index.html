The Experience

Each gathering opens and closes with clear consent-based cues and a brief coherence practice. We dim lights, settle our nervous systems, and give the night our unhurried attention.

  • Coherence practice (10–15 min)
  • Quiet observation under dark sky
  • Optional journaling & debrief
  • Accessibility-first & substance-free

What to bring

  • Warm layers / blanket
  • Notebook (optional)
  • Openness & curiosity

About

Night Under The Stars is a simple offering: practice coherence together, befriend the dark, and meet the unknown with humility. It’s not about chasing spectacle; it’s about remembering how to listen.

We hold a sovereign, non-extractive ethic—people first, stories optional, consent always.
