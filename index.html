I · The Invitation

An evening that asks something else of you.

Night Under the Stars is a small, intentional gathering. Eight people, chosen by hand, brought to a quiet place to spend a weekend together — not at an event, not at a conference, but in the kind of stretched, contemplative time that has become rare.

There is no agenda we are trying to convince you of, no apparatus you need to be wired to. The weekend opens an unhurried space for stillness, for conversation, and for the kinds of experiences that have always been available to those who slow down enough to notice.

We do not promise the phenomenon will arrive. We are simply creating the conditions that have, across many traditions and many lives, made it possible.

III · Your Hosts

Time with the people doing the work.

Jordan Jozak
Disclosure Advocate · Co‑Founder

Jordan has worked publicly as an entrepreneur for over a decade, with successful scale-ups in technology and utilities. In April 2024 he came forward as “Martin,” a pseudonymous voice on Substack writing about classified consciousness research, before revealing his identity later that year. He joined Skywatcher as a psionic asset alongside several operators who have since stepped into the public arena, and has been featured in investigative journalist Ross Coulthart’s reporting on the field. His current work brings firsthand experience of exotic technology and non-human intelligence into the open, building bridges from the disclosure domain to ordinary life. He co-founded The Telepathy Center alongside Lucas Pyle and Frank Albo, a research institution dedicated to restoring what was always ours: the ability to perceive beyond ordinary limits.

Lucas Pyle
Remote Viewer · Co‑Founder

Lucas trained in remote viewing under teachers of the Stargate-era lineage, and has spent his career on a single thread: the engineering of measurable perception. He has worked across operations, science, and the technical infrastructure of consciousness research, and served as a field operator alongside Jordan within Skywatcher. At Night Under the Stars he leads sessions in remote viewing and other contemplative practices, drawn from his own sustained practice and from the lineage he trained in. His research builds rigorous protocols for measuring telepathy, remote viewing, retrocausal influence, and other frontier phenomena. He co-founded The Telepathy Center alongside Jordan and Frank Albo, where he leads research direction, technical strategy, and experimental methodology.
